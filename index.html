About Us

Our story began long ago, in 2011, when I first gathered the courage to tell you how I felt. It wasn't easy, but something about you made it impossible for me to stay silent. When I proposed to you, you said you would think about it.

Those three months of waiting felt like an eternity. Every day, I hoped, I wondered, I dreamed. But after those three long months, you said no. It was heartbreaking, but I couldn't give up on what I felt in my heart.

After school ended, life took us in different directions. I went to Pune, and you went to Guwahati for your Bachelors. The distance was hard, but somehow, we found our way back to each other. We started talking again, and those conversations reminded me why I had never given up hope.

Every year, I would propose to you again. I knew what I wanted, and I wasn't going to let distance or time change that. Year after year, I asked, hoping that one day you would see what I saw - that we were meant to be together.

After years of waiting, after years of hoping, after years of never giving up, you finally said yes in 2017. That moment was everything I had been waiting for. Our story is one of patience, persistence, and never giving up. Through the distance, through the years of waiting, through all the challenges we've faced, one thing remained constant - my love for you.
